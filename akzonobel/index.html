bookdesign
client AkzoNobel

working with
Supervising art direction: Heleen van Gent | AkzoNobel
Concept, coordination and research: Stephanie Kranevel | AkzoNobel

Graphic design, typography and illustrations for a set of books for AkzoNobel Decorative Paints about color. Each book has the same structure in lay-out and subjects, but focuses on it's own specific color. Topics range from color-theory to interior advice.
Series contains black, white, blue, red, green, yellow, orange, purple. Published in English, Dutch, German and Portuguese.
